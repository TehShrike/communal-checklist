Communal Checklist

Create a checklist, share a link with your friends, and they can check items off the list.

Why?

My wife wanted an informal gift registry. She couldn't find anything that:

  1. Worked with whatever online retailer you wanted
  2. Let you divide set up multiple checkboxes for each item
  3. Let your friends check items off without creating an account

What you do

  1. Click the big button above
  2. Name your list
  3. Add categories to your list (optional)
  4. Add items to your list
  5. Edit items to add links to your preferred online stores
  6. Add more checkboxes to the items (in case you want six pairs of socks or something, so that different people can check as many of the checkboxes as they bought)
  7. Send the "share" link to your friends
  8. Bookmark the page so that you can edit your list later
